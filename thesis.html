Evolution of Technical Debt in Large Scale Software Systems

Bhuwan Paudel Blekinge Institute of Technology Department of Software Engineering Karlskrona, Sweden
Download Full Thesis View in DiVA Portal

Abstract (This is a preliminary version)

Technical debt (TD) has emerged as a significant research topic in software engineering, leading to considerable efforts to identify, classify, and manage it. However, empirical evidence on how TD and other architectural complexity indicators evolve in large-scale software systems remains limited. While researchers and practitioners perceive TD as a negative factor that hinders developers' performance, the actual relationship between TD and software delivery performance in an industrial context remains underexplored. Understanding these evolutions and relationships is crucial for making informed decisions regarding maintenance, refactoring, and resource allocation.
